Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Please read these terms carefully before using our services

1. Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, place an order, or contact us for support. This may include:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Billing and shipping addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment processors)
  • Custom laptop specifications and requirements

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your custom laptop orders
  • Communicate with you about orders, products, services, and promotional offers
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

2. Terms of Service

Custom Build Orders

When you place a custom laptop building order:

  • You agree to provide accurate specifications and requirements
  • Changes to orders may be subject to additional fees
  • Build timelines are estimates and may vary based on component availability
  • Deposits may be required for custom projects

Payment Terms

All prices are in US dollars unless otherwise specified. Payment is required as follows:

  • Standard products: Full payment at time of order
  • Custom builds: 50% deposit with order, balance due before shipping
  • We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers

Shipping and Delivery

Shipping costs are calculated based on weight, dimensions, and destination. We are not responsible for delays caused by shipping carriers or customs. Signature may be required for delivery.

3. Warranty and Returns

Custom Build Warranty

All custom-built laptops include:

  • 1-year limited warranty on labor and assembly
  • Manufacturer warranties on individual components
  • 90-day warranty on custom software installation and configuration

Warranty does not cover damage caused by misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications.

Return Policy

Due to the custom nature of our products:

  • Custom-built laptops are non-returnable unless defective
  • Standard laptop shells may be returned within 14 days in original condition
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer
  • Refunds will be processed within 10 business days of receiving returned items
